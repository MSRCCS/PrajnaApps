ImageDescriber

The ImageDescriber bot allows the user to find more information about the image. After they attach an image or send an image URL, they can ask the bot questions relating to what is going on in the image, emotion, and attributes of people in it (age, celebrity). For more information, ask the bot "what can you do".

Visit Bot Framework to register your bot. When you register it, remember to set your bot's endpoint to 

https://your_bots_hostname/api/messages
